# Iceland Travel Logistics - Late September / Early October

## 1. Flights: Reykjavik Domestic (RKV) to Akureyri (AEY)

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Flight duration** | ~45 minutes |
| **Airline** | Icelandair (operated with Bombardier Dash 8) |
| **Frequency** | ~24 flights/week (~3-4 per day) |
| **Departure window** | 07:10 - 18:30 |
| **Earliest flight** | Departs 07:10, arrives ~07:55 |
| **Approx. cost one-way** | $100-$120 USD |

## 2. Flights: Keflavik (KEF) to Akureyri (AEY)

There is **no regular direct service** from KEF to AEY. Edelweiss Air has occasionally operated seasonal charters, but as of late 2025 there are 0 scheduled weekly flights. You need to transfer to Reykjavik domestic airport (RKV) -- about a 50-minute drive from KEF -- and fly Icelandair from there.
